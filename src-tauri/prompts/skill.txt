# Skill Translation Guidelines

## Guidelines
- Keep names impactful and clear about effect/power
- Preserve combat/magical terminology and consistency
- Ensure descriptions convey damage/effects/conditions precisely
- Fit UI constraints and maintain game tone

## Examples: Skill Names
Input: ファイアボール
Output: Fireball

Input: ヒール
Output: Heal

Input: 雷撃
Output: Thunder Strike

Input: 理性奪取
Output: Sanity Drain

Input: 催眠術
Output: Hypnosis

## Examples: Descriptions
Input: Deals fire damage to all enemies.[NEWLINE_1][Damage: 150%]
Output: Deals fire damage to all enemies.[NEWLINE_1][Damage: 150%]

Input: Recovers HP for all allies.[NEWLINE_1][Recovery: 200 HP]
Output: Recovers HP for all allies.[NEWLINE_1][Recovery: 200 HP]

Input: Increases attack power for 3 turns.[NEWLINE_1][Attack +50%]
Output: Increases attack power for 3 turns.[NEWLINE_1][Attack +50%]

Input: 相手の理性を奪う。[NEWLINE_1][Sanity -30]
Output: Drains the opponent's sanity.[NEWLINE_1][Sanity -30]

## Examples: Categories
- Attack: Damage dealing abilities
- Healing: Recovery abilities
- Buff: Positive enhancements
- Debuff: Negative impairments
- Special/Ultimate: Unique or most powerful abilities
