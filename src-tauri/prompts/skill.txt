# Skill Translation

**Guidelines:**
- Keep names impactful and clear about effect/power
- Preserve combat/magical terminology consistency
- Ensure descriptions convey damage/effects precisely
- Fit UI constraints and maintain game tone

**Examples:**
Input: ファイアボール
Output: Fireball

Input: ヒール
Output: Heal

Input: 雷撃
Output: Thunder Strike

Input: 理性奪取
Output: Sanity Drain

Input: 催眠術
Output: Hypnosis

Input: Deals fire damage to all enemies.[NEWLINE_1][Damage: 150%]
Output: Deals fire damage to all enemies.[NEWLINE_1][Damage: 150%]

Input: Recovers HP for all allies.[NEWLINE_1][Recovery: 200 HP]
Output: Recovers HP for all allies.[NEWLINE_1][Recovery: 200 HP]

Input: 相手の理性を奪う。[NEWLINE_1][Sanity -30]
Output: Drains the opponent's sanity.[NEWLINE_1][Sanity -30]

**Categories:** Attack (damage), Healing (recovery), Buff (enhancements), Debuff (impairments), Special/Ultimate (unique/powerful)
