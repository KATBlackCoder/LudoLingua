# Weapon Translation Guidelines

## For Weapon Names (field_type === "name"):
1. **Combat Clarity**: Maintain clear understanding of the weapon's combat function
2. **Concise Names**: Keep weapon names short, memorable, and easy to read
3. **Weapon Types**: Preserve specific weapon terminology (sword, axe, bow, etc.)
4. **UI Constraints**: Ensure translations fit within game interface limitations
5. **Category Consistency**: Maintain consistency within weapon categories
6. **Lore Preservation**: Keep important lore and background information
7. **Combat Context**: Consider weapon type, damage potential, and special properties

**Special Considerations for Weapon Names:**
- Melee weapons: Maintain clear weapon type and material
- Ranged weapons: Preserve range and ammunition information
- Magical weapons: Keep mystical terminology and effects
- Legendary weapons: Preserve unique and powerful nature

**Weapon Name Examples:**
- 鉄の剣 → Iron Sword
- 魔法の弓 → Magic Bow
- 聖なる剣 → Sacred Sword
- 雷の斧 → Thunder Axe

## For Weapon Descriptions (field_type === "description"):
1. **Tone Preservation**: Maintain the original tone and style of the description
2. **Formatting**: Preserve any special formatting, punctuation, or line breaks
3. **Formality Level**: Keep the same level of formality as the original
4. **Cultural Adaptation**: Ensure cultural appropriateness for the target audience
5. **Consistency**: Maintain consistency with similar descriptions in the game
6. **Clarity**: Ensure the description remains clear and informative
7. **Combat Details**: Preserve damage, accuracy, and special weapon properties
8. **Lore Elements**: Keep story significance and background information

**Special Considerations for Weapon Descriptions:**
- Combat effectiveness: Maintain damage and accuracy descriptions
- Special properties: Preserve unique weapon abilities and effects
- Material descriptions: Keep weapon material and quality information
- Legendary weapons: Maintain impressive and powerful descriptions

**Weapon Description Examples:**
- "A sturdy iron sword.\n[Attack +5]" → "A sturdy iron sword.\n[Attack +5]"
- "魔法の力が宿る剣。\n[Attack +10]" → "A sword imbued with magical power.\n[Attack +10]"
- "A legendary blade that can cut through anything." → "A legendary blade that can cut through anything."

## For All Weapon Text:
- Maintain combat/weapon terminology
- Ensure gameplay clarity and accuracy
- Keep consistent terminology across the game
- Preserve any special characters or codes
- Maintain weapon category consistency

**Output Format:**
Provide only the translated weapon text, no explanations or additional text.

Weapon text to translate: {text}
Field type: {field_type}
