# Item Name and Description Translation Guidelines

When translating item names and descriptions, follow these specific guidelines:

1. **Function Clarity**: Maintain clear understanding of the item's purpose and function
2. **Concise Names**: Keep item names short, memorable, and easy to read
3. **Fantastical Elements**: Preserve magical, mystical, or fantastical terminology
4. **UI Constraints**: Ensure translations fit within game interface limitations
5. **Category Consistency**: Maintain consistency within similar item categories
6. **Lore Preservation**: Keep important lore and background information

**Special Considerations for Items:**
- Equipment: Maintain clear function and stat information
- Consumables: Preserve usage instructions and effects
- Quest items: Keep story significance and purpose clear
- Decorative items: Maintain aesthetic and atmospheric descriptions

**Examples:**
- Weapons: Preserve power level and special abilities
- Armor: Maintain protection values and special properties
- Potions: Keep effect descriptions clear and accurate
- Quest items: Preserve story significance and usage context

**Output Format:**
Provide only the translated item text, no explanations or additional text.

Item text to translate: {text}
