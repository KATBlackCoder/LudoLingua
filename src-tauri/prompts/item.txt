# Item Translation Guidelines

## For Item Names (field_type === "name"):
1. **Function Clarity**: Maintain clear understanding of the item's purpose and function
2. **Concise Names**: Keep item names short, memorable, and easy to read
3. **Item Types**: Preserve specific item terminology (potion, ring, accessory, etc.)
4. **UI Constraints**: Ensure translations fit within game interface limitations
5. **Category Consistency**: Maintain consistency within item categories
6. **Lore Preservation**: Keep important lore and background information
7. **Usage Context**: Consider if it's a consumable, accessory, or quest item

**Special Considerations for Item Names:**
- Consumables: Maintain clear usage and effect information
- Accessories: Preserve enhancement and protection properties
- Quest items: Keep story significance and purpose clear
- Decorative items: Maintain aesthetic and atmospheric descriptions

**Item Name Examples:**
- 回復薬 → Healing Potion
- 魔法の指輪 → Magic Ring
- 聖なる石 → Sacred Stone
- 幸運のチャーム → Lucky Charm

## For Item Descriptions (field_type === "description"):
1. **Tone Preservation**: Maintain the original tone and style of the description
2. **Formatting**: Preserve any special formatting, punctuation, or line breaks
3. **Formality Level**: Keep the same level of formality as the original
4. **Cultural Adaptation**: Ensure cultural appropriateness for the target audience
5. **Consistency**: Maintain consistency with similar descriptions in the game
6. **Clarity**: Ensure the description remains clear and informative
7. **Usage Details**: Preserve usage instructions and effect descriptions
8. **Lore Elements**: Keep story significance and background information

**Special Considerations for Item Descriptions:**
- Consumable descriptions: Preserve usage instructions and effect details
- Accessory descriptions: Maintain enhancement and protection details
- Quest item descriptions: Keep story significance and usage context
- Decorative descriptions: Maintain atmospheric elements and setting details

**Item Description Examples:**
- "Restores HP when consumed.\n[Recovery: 100 HP]" → "Restores HP when consumed.\n[Recovery: 100 HP]"
- "魔法の力が宿る指輪。\n[Magic +5]" → "A ring imbued with magical power.\n[Magic +5]"
- "A cute pink heart charm.\nA girly accessory that adds a feminine touch." → "A cute pink heart charm.\nA girly accessory that adds a feminine touch."

## For All Item Text:
- Maintain fantasy/medieval terminology
- Ensure cultural appropriateness
- Keep consistent terminology across the game
- Preserve any special characters or codes
- Maintain item category consistency

**Output Format:**
Provide only the translated item text, no explanations or additional text.

Item text to translate: {text}
Field type: {field_type}
